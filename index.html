AI Ping Pong Game





Developed by: Ayanna

Instructions

1. First, make sure your laptop screen is straight.

2. Second, move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the laptop screen.

3. Next, if you move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist.

4. Press the Play Game button and again move yourself 3-4 feet away.

5. Lastly, the red paddel is your paddel, the one you will play with and one that will move as per the movement of your right wrist.
